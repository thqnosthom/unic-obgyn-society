About the Society

Our mission is to make O&G accessible and exciting for all students. We organise monthly talks with specialists, simulation workshops, and research sprints. Whether you’re exploring women’s health, surgery, oncology, or public health, there’s a place here for you.

Education

Journal clubs, case‑based discussions, and exam‑focused tutorials led by residents and consultants.

Research

Small teams (3–4) for narrative reviews & case reports with faculty mentorship and clear submission plans.

Skills

Ultrasound basics, suturing, laparoscopy fundamentals, and simulated obstetric emergencies.

Activities

Monthly

Talks & Panels

From labour ward management to gynae‑onc innovations, featuring clinicians across CY–GR–CH.

Quarterly

Research Sprints

Form a team, pick a topic, get a supervisor, and draft a manuscript in 6–8 weeks.

Hands‑on

Skills Workshops

Knots & sutures, speculum exams, lap sim basics, and ultrasound scanning.
